# ce-compound-refresh Checklist

## What to Review
- Are existing solutions still accurate and relevant?
- Have any patterns become outdated?
- Are there duplicates or overlapping learnings?
- Is the knowledge base still high-signal?

## Actions to Take
- Update stale solutions
- Archive or merge redundant entries
- Improve searchability of important documents
- Remove noise that reduces trust in the system

## Frequency Recommendation
Run every 1–2 weeks or after a large number of new compounds have been added.
